He empowers millions of impoverished individuals, particularly women, by providing small loans to promote self-sufficiency. Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006, Yunus is celebrated for his dedication to eradicating poverty. His innovative financial model transformed lives globally, showcasing the power of social entrepreneurship to create lasting change and foster economic development in marginalized communities."
